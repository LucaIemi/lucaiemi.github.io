Luca Iemi

luca.iemi@gmail.com

I am a PhD student in cognitive neuroscience at the Berlin School of Mind and Brain and Charité Universitätsmedizin, working with Niko Busch and Felix Blankenburg. My research investigates how spontanoues brain activity influences perception. I use a combination of visual psychophysics, electroencephalography and computational modelling to understand the perceptual and neural effects of spontaneous alpha oscillations.
